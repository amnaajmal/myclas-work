Ingredients

  • Beef undercut ½ kg (sliced)
  • Orange 1
  • Orange juice ½ cup
  • Beef stock ½ cup
  • Black pepper 1 tsp (crushed)
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Oysters sauce 1 tbsp
  • Corn flour 1 tbsp
  • Vinegar 1 tbsp
  • Brown sugar 1 tbsp
  • Soya sauce 2 tbsp
  • Oil 2 – 3 tbsp
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Directions

  1. In mixing bowl mix ginger garlic paste, black pepper, soya sauce, oyster sauce, corn flour, vinegar, orange juice, brown sugar and salt.
  2. Now marinate beef from this batter.
  3. Heat oil in a grill pan and fry beef from both sides.
  4. Now in a pan add the remaining sauce and stock to cook till it become thick.
  5. Now put off the flame and heat the sizzling platter and make a layer of orange slice and put beef slices and pour sauce from top and serve it hot.
