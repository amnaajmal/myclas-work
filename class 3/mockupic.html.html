Donuts

Donut is one of the most favorit dessert among children.It have been bringing smilesince long time. Treat Yourself or spread the joy by bringing people together with a dozen donuts.Made fresh daily,and in a variety of colorful and delicious flovors.

prep time:90 minutes

Total serving:6 people

Ingredients

  • Plain flour 1 cup
  • yeast 2 tsp
  • Sugar(ground)1 cup
  • Egg1
  • Butter 1 cup
  • Lukewarm milkto knead
  • Choclate 1 cup
  • Hundreds and thousand for decoration
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Directions

  1. In a bowl,add
    • Yeast
    • Plain flour
    • Sugar
    • Egg
    • Butter

    Combine all the ingredients together and knead with milk into smooth dough.Do not warm the milk.

  2. Cover the bowl and leave it aside for half and hour.
  3. Cover the bowl and leave it aside for half and hour.
  4. Roll the dough about 1 inch thick on a floured board and cut with adoughnut cutter.
  5. Now in a large pan heat oil and fry the doughnuts until they turn golden brown.
  6. Drain in paper towels.
  7. Put the choclate into a bowl.
  8. Boil in dubble.
  9. Stir until the choclate has melted.
  10. Now dip each doughnut into the melted choclate.
  11. Dust with icing sugar and sprinkle with the hundreds and thousands
